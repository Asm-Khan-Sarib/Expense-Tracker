Welcome

Welcome to our website “Expense Tracker”. In this website you can track your expenses, so that, you don't spend more than your budget.

How to create or Update profile?

For using all the features of this website, you have to create a profile. By clicking “Create Profile” button of home page you can go to a new page, where you have to write your name and mail and click “Save” button to create profile.
If your already created a profile, you will see that “create profile” button has changed to “Update Profile”. Now can change your name and mail whenever you want.

What is this "Home Page" about?

Home page is a nice and clean starting point. From there can go to any page of this website.
There is button at the center of the page, by clicking this button, you can create and update you profile.

How can I view my expenses?

At "Transactions" page, there is a drop-down button name "Show All Transections", from here you can choose how you would like to orient your transections.
There are two options here, first one is "Sort by Time" and second one is "Sort bt Category". By selecting any of these, we can see full list of your expenses.

How can I see the chart?

At "Reports" page, there are three buttons. First one for "Doughnut Chart", second one for "Bar Chart" and third one for "Remove Chart".

How can I add expenses?

At "Add Expences" page, you will find a form to write information about your expenses. You have to write the the amount you spend, date, small discription (this is optional) and select a category.
After filling the form, you just need to click the "Save" button. Your expenses will be saved and you will see a confirmation massege.

How can I delet expenses?

At "Delet Expences" page, there are two options for deleting your saved expenses. First one is delet one expense manually and second one is quick delet.
For first one, you have to write the transaction id (in transection list, left side number is transaction id) which you want to delet and click the "Delet" button. Your expense will be deleted and you will get a confirmation massage.
For second one, there are three buttons, by clicking these buttons you can quickly delet your expenses.

Why I can't click any buttons?

You have to create a profile to use this website.
